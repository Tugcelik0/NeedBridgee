Frequently asked

What happens after the 3-month free period?
+
After October 2026, Seekers always post for free. Solvers keep a permanent free Explorer tier (3 applications/month). Paid plans unlock unlimited applications, pipeline tools, and verified badges. All data and profiles are preserved from the free period.
Is there a commission on deals?
+
Yes — a 1.5% success commission paid by the Solver when a deal closes. Growth plan subscribers pay 1%. For deals under €100K, a minimum flat fee of €1,500 applies. Seekers pay zero commission — ever.
How does the commitment deposit work?
+
Seekers can optionally deposit €500–€2,000 when posting a problem. If they complete at least 2 meetings with applicants within 90 days, the deposit is fully refunded. If not, NeedBridge retains it — protecting Solvers from dead listings.
Do technopark accounts cost anything?
+
Technopark and accelerator partner accounts are free or heavily subsidised depending on portfolio size and geographic region. Apply through the partner programme — decisions within 5 business days.
