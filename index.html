Describe what you need — software, hardware, machine, cleantech system, or anything in between. Set your budget range and deadline. Your company name stays anonymous.

✓ Define roles: Listing Owner + Budget Approver

03

04

Agree on pilot scope, duration, and payment. The pilot fee is held in platform escrow and released automatically when the startup delivers. Both sides protected.

Platform trust

Built so neither side
can game the system.

Five interlocking layers make bypassing the platform more costly than staying — for everyone.

🎭
Proposal anonymity
The company sees your solution pitch — not your name or website. Your identity is only revealed after they formally approve a meeting request on-platform.
→ The introduction happens on NeedBridge
📋
Meeting records
Every meeting is logged — date, time, participants. This creates legal proof that both parties were introduced through NeedBridge. Covered by a 24-month contract clause.
→ Burden of proof reverses
🔒
Pilot escrow
Pilot payment is held in platform escrow. Released automatically when the startup delivers. Neither side takes payment risk.
→ Deliver first, payment follows
⏱️
90-day action rule
After budget discussion, automated alerts at 30/60/90 days. At day 90 with no action, the listing suspends and the commitment deposit is forfeited.
→ Dead listings cost money
⭐
Solver value lock
Verified badge, reference score, and priority matching are platform-exclusive. A solver who bypasses the platform loses this accumulated reputation permanently.
→ Long-term cost of leaving
🔍
Anomaly detection
System flags unusual patterns — a company viewing a profile 5+ times without acting, or a solver later listing that company as a client after an "unsuccessful" close.
→ Commission claim triggered
